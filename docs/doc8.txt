Conclusion
Blockchain is a revolutionizing technology but unlike any other new technology, it has its’ limitations and issues. Slow speed and complexity for the end user being major ones, because people want that things are done fast, and as easily as possible. An everyday user right now can’t imagine himself paying for coffee or a quick snack with a blockchain based cryptocurrency. Certain things are to be brought in order before it reaches everyone and changes the way we handle and use information on the internet.
